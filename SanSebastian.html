San Sebastian

Things to do in San Sebastian:

Private Sailing at La Concha Beach

Sail on a private cruise! Go sightseeing in peace and explore the beautiful La Concha Beach! Allow the captain to navigate you to amazing coastal scenery from a unique vantage point. You will also have the opportunity to explore the charming Basque Fishing Villages of Pasai Donibane.

Learn More

Eco Farm Tour and Food Tasting

Tour local farms filled with tons of farm animals! Meet some of their farm animals like cows! Learn about local San Sebastian farms and even taste some local dishes.

Learn More

Pintxo Tasting Paired with Spanish Wines

Journey into the quaint old town of San Sebastian known as San Sebastian! Try out many local cuisine and immerse yourself with the locals. If of age, you can try out som of the local Spanish Wines! You can also learn about the wonderous history of Parte Vieja.

Learn More

French Basque Coast Tour

Take a tour away from the crowded city along the French Basque coast from San Sebastian. Visit the quaint city of Bayonne and a fishing village of Saint Jean de Luz. Tour the beach and learn about local cuisines! Offers limited group sizes for an intimate experience.
